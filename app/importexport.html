Import & Export

Luminous Impex has been part of the SeaFood export industry for the last 15 years. We strive and achieve the highest in quality and precision of results. Connecting different hemispheres and thereby many different countries and continents, we are everywhere.

We have strong links with renowned sources for inputs and ingredients to deliver the best of service to our valued clients and customers. We bring in flavours and colors from the best of places to provide consistently a package for all the people who are connected to us.

All that we do also includes these -

  • Clearing, Forwarding & Logistics
  • Shipping services(Bulk and individual)
  • Documentation and Customs Clearance, Cargo Insurance
  • Bonded warehousing, Packing and Unpacking
  • Container consolidation and de-conslidation
  • Shipment and clearing of Personal Effects

Luminous measures the time and cost (excluding tariffs) associated with three sets of procedures—documentary compliance, border compliance and domestic transport—within the overall process of exporting or importing a shipment of goods.

Extensive research and intensive verification are the key focus areas that we continually strive to keep everything in place. Working with over a dozen different agencies, we gather an expanse of knowledge and in-depth analysis of Supply and Demand.Severely making efforts to be more effective and showcase ourselves as a niche brand in the service sector.
